About us

The Society for the Appreciation and Acceptance of Gingers, or SAAG, was founded in 1855 in order to promote the love and fair treatment of red-haired, light skinned people, known commonly as "gingers". Our mission is to promote acceptance and understanding of our pigment-challenged bretheren through research, education, political activism.

Please take a look around! If you would like to support our cause, we accept donations of all varieties, with a strong preference for cash. Unfortunately, because gingers are not yet federally recognized as a protected class, we are currently unable to provide receipts for tax purposes.

If you are red-haired yourself, check out the shop! We provide links to many convenient and necessary products to help you make your way in an unfriendly world.
